BuddySync Privacy Policy

BuddySync Learning Network Inc. ("BuddySync," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the BuddySync mobile app (“App”) and our services.

Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal data to provide our services and improve user experience:

Personal Data

  • Name
  • Email Address (via signup or Google/Apple Sign-In)
  • Message exchanged with users
  • Profile Information (avatar, study preferences, location, course tags)
  • Usage Data
  • Device info (model, OS version, IP address)
  • Usage logs, error reports
  • Analytics
  • Non-identifying usage metrics to improve performance and features

Why we collect these: To match users, enable Pomodoro sessions and messaging, maintain app performance, and ensure platform stability and security.

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve service features, such as matching, chat, and Pomodoro
  • Security & debugging to detect and fix issues
  • Analytics to understand usage patterns
  • Legal compliance if required by law

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal data. We may disclose it to:

  • Service providers (e.g., Firebase, analytics tools)
  • When required by law or to prevent harm
  • With your consent

All service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Google Sign-In & API Complianc

BuddySync uses Google Sign-In (and obtains your email and profile info) in accordance with Google's API Services User Data Policy. We include this clause as required:

BuddySync’s use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Safety & Permissions

In compliance with Google and Apple policies, we declare:

  • Personal Data (Name, Email, Location, Profile)
  • Messages (User-generated)
  • Device Info (OS version, IP)
  • Analytics data

These are used solely for app functionality, security, and improvement.

Children’s Privacy

BuddySync is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Any accounts discovered under this age will be deleted.

Retention & Security

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account anytime. After deletion, personal data is permanently removed within 30 days unless legally required. We use industry-standard security, such as encrypted storage and secure network connections.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the data we hold
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account/data
  • Revoke consent for Google access
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on it

To make a request, email support@buddysync.app.

Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be announced in-app or emailed, with at least 30 days’ notice. Continued use following changes means you accept them.
