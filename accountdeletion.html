You can permanently delete your BuddySync account at any time directly from within the app.

To delete your account:

  1. Open the BuddySync app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm your choice

Important Information

Immediate deletion: When you delete your account, all your data (profile, messages, tags, preferences etc.) will be permanently removed from our systems immediately.

This action cannot be undone: You won't be able to recover any of your data after deletion.
